Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

cookie

We respect your privacy. Literally.

The Axis Gamepad Pro website (axisgamepad.com) currently uses zero cookies, zero trackers, and zero analytics scripts.

01 Why no cookies?

Our website is designed to be a fast, informative portal—not a data collection tool. We don't employ remarketing tags, cross-site trackers, or even basic analytics right now. You can verify this by checking your browser's dev tools; the storage and cookies panel will be empty.

02 What about the Android App?

The Axis Gamepad Pro Android app itself manages its own necessary local state (like saved profiles) securely on your device. It does not use web-based "cookies." For details on data related to the app, AdMob advertising, or Google Play purchases, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

03 Future Changes

If we ever decide to implement basic operational cookies or analytics in the future to improve website performance or understand traffic trends, we will update this document and present a compliant cookie consent banner to all our visitors first.
