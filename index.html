Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get banned for using Axis? expand_more
Axis uses system-level touch injection that is identical to real hardware touch signals. It does not modify game files or inject code into the game process, making it significantly safer than traditional overlay mappers.
Which controllers are supported? expand_more
Any controller that is recognized as an HID device by Android will work. This includes all modern Xbox, PlayStation, and 8BitDo controllers, as well as many mobile-specific gamepads like Razer Kishi and Gamesir.
Is Root really not required? expand_more
Correct. By leveraging Shizuku, Axis can gain the necessary system permissions to inject touch events without needing a full device root. This keeps your device secure and your banking apps working.
How much input lag should I expect? expand_more
Our injection engine is optimized for sub-2ms processing time. Your total latency will mostly depend on your controller's Bluetooth connection (typically 4-12ms). For competitive play, USB-C connection is recommended.
